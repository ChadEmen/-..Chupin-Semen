Biography

Since early childhood, I have been passionate about drawing, starting from kindergarten and school, where I drew various scenes on my desks. My talent came from my mother, who was an artist and worked in the airport creating beautiful signs and painting portraits. My father, a civil aviation pilot, also worked there. After school, I entered university (1997-2002) and immediately fell in love with painting. My teacher, Chilikhin at Khudgaf, when he saw that I chose pencil drawing for my diploma, cried and said: "You are a wonderful painter, and it is a pity you chose only drawing!" Later, he passed away, but his words stayed with me. Since 2002, I have devoted myself to painting, winning first places for color compositions during my studies. I created a series of oil paintings that caused a sensation at exhibitions in Kursk. Each painting took months, with my soul poured into it. Visitors often cried seeing my landscapes. I tried various businesses but could not live without painting. It has been my life for over 22 years. Traveling, especially to Vietnam, inspired me to capture the beauty of our planet on canvas. Few timely words can change a person's destiny! I realized how the education system suppresses talents and complicates what is naturally simple. My course reveals the true secrets of creativity, bypassing unnecessary techniques and false teachings, letting the creative energy flow freely.
