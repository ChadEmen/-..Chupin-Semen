Welcome to my biography!

Since early childhood, I have been passionate about drawing, starting from kindergarten and school, where I drew various scenes on my desks. My talent was inherited from my mother, who was an artist and worked in the airport creating beautiful signs and painting portraits. My father also worked there as a civil aviation pilot. After school, I entered the university (1997-2002) and immediately became interested in painting. My teacher, Chilikhin at Khudgaf, when seeing my diploma choice as pencil drawing, cried and said: "You are a wonderful painter, and it's a pity you chose only drawing!" Later, he passed away, but his words stayed with me. Since 2002, I have been devoted to painting, winning first places for color composition during my studies. I created a series of oil paintings that caused a sensation at exhibitions in Kursk. Each painting took months, with my soul poured into them. Visitors often cried seeing my landscapes. I tried different businesses but could not live without painting, which has been my life for over 22 years. Traveling, especially to Vietnam, inspired me to capture the beauty of our planet on canvas. Few timely words can change a person's destiny! I realized how the education system suppresses talents and complicates what is naturally simple. My course reveals the true secrets, bypassing unnecessary techniques and false teachings, letting creativity flow freely.
